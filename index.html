NMT Experiments Homepage

Below are all of my New Media & Tech projects for this year.

Projects

Black Box Experiment

We explored a mystery container that changed the amount poured into it. By running trials and collecting data, we worked to uncover the hidden contraption inside

Our Shared Canvas

Using art to send a message and fix a problem in our community. We worked with screenprinting to create art pieces that addressed an issue in our community and documented the entire process in a video.

DNA Experiment

We investigated how genetics and society shape ideas about identity. We extracted and analyzed our DNA using pcr and gel electrophoresis to compare to our classmates then documented everything in a short video.

About This Site

This website has all of my New Media & Tech experiments, including screen printing, digital photography, Photoshop, Maya 3D modeling, and DNA extraction. Each project page includes photos, data, models, and reflections that show my learning process throughout the year.
